# 001 - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

> Source of truth: [`docs/sekpriAI_Source_of_Truth_Blueprint.md`](../docs/sekpriAI_Source_of_Truth_Blueprint.md) §2.
> Treat this PRD as the implementation contract. Specs win over CLAUDE.md if
> they ever disagree; if specs and the blueprint disagree, fix the spec.

## 1. Product summary

sekpriAI is an AI-first universal email client delivered as a mobile-ready
PWA. It connects real Gmail, Office 365, and IMAP providers (Yahoo, AOL,
custom) into one unified inbox. On top of standard email actions, sekpriAI
adds an AI secretary that summarizes, prioritizes, drafts replies, classifies
risk, extracts memory candidates, performs RAG over email content and
attachments, sends high-priority notifications through Telegram, and supports
a full mock WhatsApp command flow.

## 2. Goals

- Polished mobile-ready PWA deployed to Vercel (free tier).
- Real provider connections: Gmail OAuth, Office 365 OAuth, IMAP/SMTP for
  Yahoo, AOL, and custom hosts.
- Unified inbox with account switching.
- Standard mail actions: compose, reply, forward, search, labels, archive,
  delete.
- AI features: summary, reply draft, priority classification, risk
  classification, suggested next action.
- AI memory pipeline with pending/active/rejected/deleted states and explicit
  user approval before activation.
- RAG over email body plus PDF, TXT, DOCX attachments.
- Telegram bot for notifications and natural-language email commands.
- WhatsApp mock command UI without real WhatsApp API.
- Specs-driven development with role-based agents, read/change/verify loop,
  and automated tests.

## 3. Non-goals

The MVP intentionally excludes:

- Calendar.
- Contacts manager.
- Tasks.
- Notes.
- CRM.
- Real WhatsApp Business API integration.
- Autonomous high-risk email sending.
- Enterprise-grade production sync (queues, multi-region, SLAs).

If a contributor proposes any of the above, update the PRD first or reject
the change.

## 4. Personas

- **Solo professional** with multiple inboxes (work Gmail, personal IMAP,
  university Office 365) who wants one place to triage email and offload
  drafting to an AI assistant.
- **Power user on mobile** who relies on Telegram for fast replies and
  notifications when away from a laptop.

## 6. Success criteria

- A demo user can connect at least one Gmail and one IMAP account and see a
  unified inbox.
- AI summary, priority badge, and reply draft render for each message within
  a few seconds of opening.
- A send action requires explicit confirmation; risky categories require
  careful approval.
- Telegram binding works end to end on a real bot, and a high-priority email
  triggers a notification within a few seconds of sync.
- WhatsApp mock chat accepts commands and produces parsed responses.
- Memory items extracted by AI never become active without user approval.
- The app is usable on a mid-range mobile device in portrait orientation.
- All quality gates (lint, typecheck, unit, integration, E2E, build) pass on
  the deployed branch.

## 7. Safety requirements (binding)

- AI may summarize, draft, classify priority, classify risk, and extract
  memory candidates automatically.
- AI may **not** activate a memory item without user approval.
- AI may **not** send any email without explicit user approval.
- Emails involving business decisions, payment, legal, contracts, pricing,
  client approval, confidential data, or complaints require careful approval
  with the draft visible to the user before send.
- All tokens, API keys, and bot secrets stay server-side. None reach the
  browser bundle.

## 8. Out-of-scope clarifications

- No real-time presence, typing indicators, or read receipts.
- No team / shared inbox features.
- No paid tiers, billing, or quota management.

## 9. Open questions

- Final choice of email layout density (Gmail default vs comfortable). Track
  in `specs/002-design-spec.md`.
- Whether to expose AI provider switching to end users or keep it env-only.
  Default: env-only for the MVP.
